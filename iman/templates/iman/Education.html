Dissertation: A New Approach for Density-Based Clustering

Project descriptionA novel method of clustering which defines a density level for each data point with respect to the effect of other points on them. According to the exerted densities, it generates a directed graph from the dataset. The algorithm can predict the number and also the shapes of clusters and visualize them so as to be interpreted by experts.
